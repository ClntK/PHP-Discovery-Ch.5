The Chinese Zodiac. Known by the Chinese as Sheng Xiao or Shu Xiang. It has 12 signs. They are:
Rat 
Ox 
Tiger 
Rabbit 
Dragon 
Snake 
Horse 
Sheep 
Monkey 
Rooster 
Dog 
Pig
The Chinese Zodiac is more than 2,000 years old.
The Chinese Year is broken up into 10 heavenly stems: 
Jia 
Yi 
Bing 
Ding 
Wu 
Ji 
Geng 
Xin 
Ren 
Gui
And 12 earthly branches which are represented by the signs listed above.
